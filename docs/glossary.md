# Glossary: Digital Clinical Trial Architecture Terminology

## Terms A–M

**ADaM** – Analysis Data Model; CDISC standard for analysis-ready datasets derived from SDTM, with explicit traceability requirements.

**AE / SAE** – Adverse Event / Serious Adverse Event; safety events reported and tracked throughout a trial.

**ALCOA+** – Data integrity framework: Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate, plus Complete, Consistent, Enduring, Available.

**CAPA** – Corrective and Preventive Action; formal quality process for addressing root causes of deviations/errors.

**CDASH** – Clinical Data Acquisition Standards Harmonization; CDISC standard governing data collection field design.

**CDISC** – Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium; publishes CDASH, SDTM, ADaM and related standards.

**CFR Part 11** – 21 CFR Part 11; FDA regulation governing electronic records and electronic signatures.

**CRO** – Contract Research Organization; third-party vendor managing trial operations on behalf of a sponsor.

**CSA** – Computer Software Assurance; FDA's risk-based alternative approach to traditional CSV.

**CSV** – Computer System Validation; traditional documented validation methodology for GxP computerized systems.

**CtQ (Critical to Quality)** – Data/process factors most likely to affect subject safety or result reliability if compromised.

**CTMS** – Clinical Trial Management System; operational system tracking site management, enrollment, monitoring, budgets.

**Define.xml** – Machine-readable metadata file describing the structure/content of submitted SDTM/ADaM datasets.

**DCT** – Decentralized Clinical Trial; trial model reducing reliance on traditional in-person site visits.

**DHT** – Digital Health Technology; FDA terminology for sensors, wearables, and software used to capture trial data.

**DMP** – Data Management Plan; document specifying data handling, source-of-truth rules, and quality processes.

**DSMB** – Data Safety Monitoring Board; independent committee reviewing accumulating safety data during a trial.

**DtP** – Direct-to-Patient; shipment model delivering investigational product directly to a patient's home.

**eCOA** – electronic Clinical Outcome Assessment; umbrella term for electronic PRO/ClinRO/ObsRO/PerfO instruments.

**eConsent** – Electronic informed consent process, often including interactive comprehension elements.

**EDC** – Electronic Data Capture; central system for clinical trial data entry.

**eIDAS** – EU regulation governing electronic identification and trust services, including e-signatures.

**ePRO** – electronic Patient-Reported Outcome; subset of eCOA capturing patient-reported data.

**eTMF** – electronic Trial Master File; regulatory document repository for a clinical trial.

**FDA BIMO** – FDA's Bioresearch Monitoring program; conducts inspections of trial sites, sponsors, and IRBs.

**FHIR** – Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources; HL7 standard for real-time healthcare data exchange.

**GCP** – Good Clinical Practice; international ethical/scientific quality standard for human subjects research (ICH E6).

**GDPR** – General Data Protection Regulation; EU data privacy law.

**GxP** – Umbrella term for "Good [X] Practice" regulations (GCP, GMP, GLP, etc.) governing regulated industries.

**HL7** – Health Level Seven; healthcare data exchange standards organization.

**ICH E6** – International Council for Harmonisation guideline defining Good Clinical Practice; R2 (2016) and R3 (2023+) are key revisions.

**IQ/OQ/PQ** – Installation/Operational/Performance Qualification; traditional CSV validation stage terminology.

**IRT / IWRS** – Interactive Response Technology / Interactive Web Response System; alternate names for RTSM.

## Terms N–Z

**Part 11** – See CFR Part 11.

**PGHD** – Patient-Generated Health Data; data originating from patient-owned devices/platforms.

**QTL** – Quality Tolerance Limit; pre-specified study-level threshold for acceptable variation in critical processes.

**RACT** – Risk Assessment and Categorization Tool; framework for structured trial risk assessment.

**RBQM** – Risk-Based Quality Management; quality approach focusing oversight on critical-to-quality risk factors.

**RTSM** – Randomization and Trial Supply Management; system managing randomization, blinding, and drug supply logistics.

**RWD / RWE** – Real-World Data / Real-World Evidence; data/evidence derived outside traditional RCT settings.

**SAP** – Statistical Analysis Plan; pre-specified document detailing planned statistical analyses.

**SCC** – Standard Contractual Clauses; EU-approved mechanism for lawful cross-border data transfer.

**SDTM** – Study Data Tabulation Model; CDISC standard for submission-ready tabulated clinical data.

**SDV** – Source Data Verification; process of confirming EDC data against original source documents.

**TLF** – Tables, Listings, and Figures; standard outputs of statistical analysis for clinical study reports.

**TMF** – Trial Master File (see eTMF).

**UAT** – User Acceptance Testing; validation testing performed by end users before system go-live.

**URS/FRS** – User Requirements Specification / Functional Requirements Specification; validation documentation artifacts.

**USDM** – Unified Study Definitions Model; emerging standard for machine-readable protocol digitization.

**WCAG** – Web Content Accessibility Guidelines; standard for accessible digital interface design.
