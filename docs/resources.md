# Resources: Further Reading & Tools for Digital Clinical Trial Architects

## Key Guidelines & Standards
- **ICH E6(R2)/(R3)** — Good Clinical Practice guidelines (ich.org)
- **21 CFR Part 11** — FDA electronic records/signatures regulation (ecfr.gov)
- **FDA Computer Software Assurance (CSA) Draft Guidance** (2022) — risk-based validation approach
- **FDA Digital Health Technologies for Remote Data Acquisition Guidance** (2023)
- **CDISC Standards** (cdisc.org) — CDASH, SDTM, ADaM, TAUGs, USDM
- **HL7 FHIR** (hl7.org/fhir) — healthcare interoperability standard
- **EU Annex 11** — EU GMP guideline on computerized systems

## Textbooks & References
- **"Principles and Practice of Clinical Trial Medicine"** (Chin & Lee) — clinical trial operations foundations
- **"Clinical Data Management"** (Krishnankutty et al.) — practical data management reference
- **"Decentralized Clinical Trials: A Practical Guide"** (various industry publications, DTRA/ACRO resources)

## Organizations
- **DTRA (Decentralized Trials & Research Alliance)** — DCT best practices and industry standards
- **TransCelerate BioPharma** — cross-industry clinical development process standardization
- **CDISC** — clinical data standards development
- **SCDM (Society for Clinical Data Management)** — data management professional body
- **ACRP / DIA** — clinical research professional associations with relevant conferences/publications

## Regulatory Resources
- FDA guidance documents (fda.gov/regulatory-information)
- EMA guidance on DCTs and computerized systems (ema.europa.eu)
- ICH official guidelines (ich.org)
